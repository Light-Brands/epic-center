# Programs & Ministry Records

Charitable programs, worship service logs, and ministry documentation.

## Structure

| Subdirectory | Description | Frequency |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| `worship-services/` | Weekly worship service attendance and documentation | Weekly |
| `charitable-programs/` | Charitable program records and impact data | Ongoing |
| `education/` | Educational programs and curriculum delivery | Ongoing |
| `community/` | Community outreach and service records | Ongoing |

## IRS 14-Point Test Relevance

Regular worship services and charitable programs are critical to maintaining your church status under the IRS 14-point test. Living Light OS tracks:

- **Weekly worship services** with attendance counts
- **Regular congregation** evidence
- **Charitable program** delivery and impact
- **Educational activities** and curriculum use
- **Minister duties** and religious functions performed
