## Summary of Findings

The luxury Tulum and Akumal compound tier is anchored at **USD $11,900,000** by two independent Christie's International Real Estate Mexico listings at the same price point:

| Property | Asking | Bedrooms | Location | Brokerage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Rancho Paraiso Oasis** (subject) | $11,900,000 | 16 | Akumal corridor | Christie's International Real Estate |
| **Casa Luna** | $11,900,000 | 13 | Region 15, Tulum | Christie's International Real Estate Mexico |

Both properties are luxury wellness-oriented compounds with multi-bedroom capacity, comparable amenity stacks, and similar market positioning. The convergence of two Christie's listings at exactly $11,900,000 establishes a tight market anchor for this price tier and geography.

A 128-hectare raw beachfront parcel in Akumal is separately listed at $30,000,000, providing the upper boundary of the Akumal market at institutional mega-parcel scale.

---

## Methodology

Mexican real estate transactions do not publish closed-sale pricing through any public registry. Market comparables in this jurisdiction are assessed using:

1. Active public listings from internationally recognized luxury brokerages (Christie's International Real Estate, Sotheby's International Realty, Engel and Völkers)
2. Price convergence across multiple independent listings at the same price tier
3. Brokerage-reported pricing bands for specific geographic and product segments

Asking prices are the primary signal in the Mexican luxury segment. A formal third-party appraisal ("avalúo comercial") can be commissioned from a licensed Mexican appraiser during diligence to support the valuation anchor documented here.

## Valuation Anchor

**Market Value Reference:** USD $11,900,000

This reference is supported by:

1. **Direct subject listing** at $11,900,000 on Christie's International Real Estate (ID 047661) and three additional luxury listing platforms.
2. **Independent comparable** Casa Luna at $11,900,000 on Christie's International Real Estate Mexico, at the same price point in the same brokerage channel.
3. **Per-bedroom extrapolation** from Casa Luna ($915,000 to $992,000 per bedroom) applied to the subject's 16 bedrooms yields $14.6M to $15.9M, suggesting room for upward revision on a per-bedroom basis.

**Construction Capital Reference (Replacement Cost):** USD $25,000,000+

Over $25 million of prior capital has been deployed into the Property across land, construction, and soft costs. This figure is retained as a replacement-cost reference separate from the market-value anchor. It describes the capital required to rebuild the Property from greenfield, not the current transfer value.

## Valuation Range

| Scenario | Range |

A formal appraisal during diligence will anchor to a specific number within or adjacent to these ranges. The $11.9M market anchor is the most defensible single figure based on current public data.
