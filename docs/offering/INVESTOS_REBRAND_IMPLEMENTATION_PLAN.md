## Name Change Rationale

### Why InvestOS (Not OSinvest)

**Linguistic Flow:**
- "InvestOS" reads as "Investment Operating System"
- Follows established OS naming conventions (macOS, iOS, ChromeOS)
- "OS" suffix is more recognizable as "Operating System"

**Brand Identity:**
- Clearer product category signal
- Easier to trademark and defend
- More professional/technical positioning
- Better domain availability (investos.com vs osinvest.com)

**Marketing:**
- "Built on InvestOS" (like "Built on Shopify")
- "InvestOS-powered" (like "AWS-powered")
- Platform thinking vs service thinking
