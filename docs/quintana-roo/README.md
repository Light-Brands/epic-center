## Key Criteria Reminders

### Must-Have (Pass/Fail Gates)
1. JCI hospital ≤30 min with cardiac cath lab
2. ≥5 existing bedrooms or space for 8+ rooms
3. Proper zoning
4. Clear title
5. Safe security zone (Level 1-2)
6. Utilities available
7. Ambulance-accessible road
8. Structurally sound

### Scoring Categories (100 Points)
- Medical Infrastructure: 20 pts
- Location & Accessibility: 15 pts
- Property & Building: 15 pts
- Privacy & Security: 12 pts
- Regulatory & Legal: 10 pts
- Infrastructure & Utilities: 8 pts
- Financial: 8 pts
- Natural Environment: 6 pts
- Risk Factors: 4 pts
- Strategic Fit: 2 pts

## Target Zones

### Optimal (If Hospital Verified)
- **Tulum** - Airport + Tren Maya + wellness positioning (VERIFY hospital access)

### Optimal (Confirmed)
- **Puerto Morelos** - Quiet, central, 25-35 min to Cancun hospitals
- **Playa del Carmen North** - Full services, 10 min to Hospital Playa
- **Playacar** - Gated, upscale, 5-10 min to hospital

### Acceptable
- **Akumal** - Beautiful but 50-60 min to Cancun hospitals
- **Playa del Carmen South** - Good value, developing

### Avoid
- **Hotel Zone Cancun** - Too commercial, no privacy
- **Far South (Sian Ka'an)** - Too remote from medical
