- Regulatory filings (SEC, etc.)
- Novel business models requiring judgment
- High-stakes M&A (>$100M transactions)
- Situations requiring professional liability coverage

### 5.2 When to Use AI + Professional Review

- Investor pitch materials ✓
- Internal decision-making valuations ✓
- Due diligence support ✓

- Preliminary valuations for negotiations ✓
