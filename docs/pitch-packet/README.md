## Why This Approach?

1. **The Pitch IS the Product Demo** - Shows our technical capability
2. **Stands Out** - Interactive experience vs another PDF
3. **Scales Outreach** - One portal, unlimited investors
4. **Tracks Engagement** - Know who's interested and what they're reading
5. **Reduces Friction** - Self-service data room + easy scheduling
