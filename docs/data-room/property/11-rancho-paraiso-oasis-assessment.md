### 4.2 Acquisition Notes

- **Fideicomiso:** Required trust structure for foreign ownership of Mexican real estate within the restricted zone (100 km from borders, 50 km from coastline). Annual maintenance approximately $2,000-3,000/year.
- **Currency Hedge:** Forward contract to lock USD/MXN exchange rate between letter of intent and closing to mitigate peso volatility.
- **Due Diligence:** Includes environmental assessment, structural survey, title search, zoning verification, and boundary survey across all 9 lots.
- **Title Insurance:** Protects against defects in title, liens, or encumbrances discovered post-closing.
