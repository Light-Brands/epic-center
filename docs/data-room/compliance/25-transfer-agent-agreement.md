### 1.1 Background

Transformational Epicenter Holdings, LLC is conducting a capital formation:

| Track | Regulation | Security | Status |
|-------|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Reg D** | Rule 506(b) or 506(c) [Pending counsel determination] | [To be determined -- LLC Membership Interests / Preferred Units / SAFE] | Pre-offering -- structuring phase |

The Company's total raise target is $17,323,500. Upon commencement of the offering, the Company will require a Transfer Agent to:

- Maintain the official registry of members/unit holders
- Process securities issuances under Reg D
- Manage transfer restrictions and legends
- Support ongoing reporting requirements

### 1.2 Regulatory Requirements

- **Section 17A of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934** requires Transfer Agents to register with the SEC
- **SEC Rule 17Ad** series governs Transfer Agent operations
- The Transfer Agent must be registered with the SEC and, if a bank, with the appropriate banking regulator
- The Transfer Agent must maintain books and records as required by Rule 17Ad-7

### 1.3 Coordination with Broker-Dealer

[To be designated] broker-dealer will serve as the Company's FINRA-registered broker-dealer for the offering. The Transfer Agent must coordinate with the broker-dealer for:

- Investor subscription processing and settlement
- Securities issuance upon subscription acceptance
- Compliance verification confirmation
- Ongoing reporting data

## 3. Scope of Services

### 3.1 Core Transfer Agent Services

The Transfer Agent will provide the following services:

**Member/Unit Holder Administration:**
- Maintain the official register of members/unit holders
- Process new securities issuances (membership interests, preferred units, or SAFE instruments)
- Process securities transfers (subject to transfer restrictions)
- Process securities cancellations
- Maintain and update restrictive legends
- Issue replacement certificates or DRS statements
- Process member/unit holder address and name changes

**Corporate Actions:**
- Process unit splits and reverse splits (if applicable)
- Process distributions
- Process conversions (SAFE-to-equity, if applicable)
- Support entity reorganizations
- Process member votes and consent solicitations

**Compliance Support:**
- Maintain transfer restriction records (Rule 144, Reg D)
- Monitor holding periods for restricted securities
- Issue legal opinions for legend removal (in coordination with counsel)
- Support blue sky filing compliance
- Provide member/unit holder data for regulatory reporting

**Technology & Investor Portal:**
- Online investor portal for member/unit holder access
- Real-time cap table management
- Digital certificates or DRS statements
- Investor self-service (address changes, tax document access)
- Integration with Company's investor relations platform

## 5. Transfer Processing

### 5.1 Transfer Restrictions

| Security | Restriction | Holding Period | Legend |
|----------|------------|----------------|--------|
| **Reg D Membership Interests / Units** | Restricted -- Rule 144 | 12 months from issuance | Restricted legend required |
| **Founder/Officer Interests** | Restricted -- Rule 144 affiliate | Ongoing (affiliate status) | Restricted legend required |
| **SAFE Instruments** | Non-transferable until conversion | Until conversion event | Restricted legend required |

### 5.2 Transfer Procedures

**For Restricted Securities:**
1. Transfer request received by Transfer Agent
2. Transfer Agent verifies holding period and restriction status
3. If restricted, legal opinion required from Company counsel before transfer
4. Transfer Agent processes transfer upon receipt of satisfactory legal opinion
5. New certificate/DRS statement issued with appropriate legend

**For Securities with Expired Restriction Period:**
1. Transfer request received by Transfer Agent
2. Transfer Agent verifies holding period has expired and no additional restrictions apply
3. Legal opinion obtained confirming eligibility for legend removal
4. Transfer Agent processes transfer
5. New certificate/DRS statement issued without legend (or with updated legend)

### 5.3 Lost Securities

- Transfer Agent maintains affidavit and indemnity bond procedures for lost certificates
- Open penalty surety bond required for replacement (typically 2% of current value, minimum $50)
- 30-day waiting period before replacement issuance (unless bond posted)

## 7. Securities Conversion Processing

### 7.1 Conversion Mechanics

If the Company issues SAFE instruments that later convert to membership interests or units, the Transfer Agent must support:

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Conversion trigger** | As specified in SAFE agreement [To be determined] |
| **Conversion terms** | Per SAFE instrument terms [To be determined] |
| **Securities class** | [To be determined -- LLC Membership Interests / Preferred Units] |

### 7.2 Conversion Process

1. **Trigger event** -- Company notifies Transfer Agent that conversion event has occurred
2. **SAFE holder list** -- Company provides Transfer Agent with complete SAFE holder register including:
   - SAFE holder name and identification
   - Principal amount
   - Applicable conversion terms
   - Total units/interests to be issued
3. **Conversion calculation verification** -- Transfer Agent (or Company counsel) verifies conversion mathematics
4. **Securities issuance** -- Transfer Agent issues membership interests/units to each converting SAFE holder
5. **Legend application** -- Converted securities receive Rule 144 restricted legend (12-month holding period from conversion date)
6. **SAFE cancellation** -- Company confirms SAFEs cancelled upon conversion
7. **Cap table update** -- Transfer Agent updates cap table to reflect all conversions
8. **Investor notification** -- Transfer Agent issues DRS statements to converted SAFE holders

## 10. Data Security & Privacy

### 10.1 Requirements

The Transfer Agent must maintain:

- **SOC 2 Type II certification** (or equivalent) for data security
- **Encryption** of all member/unit holder PII in transit and at rest
- **Access controls** limiting data access to authorized personnel
- **Business continuity** and disaster recovery plans
- **Breach notification** procedures (within 24 hours of discovery)
- **Compliance** with applicable state privacy laws (e.g., CCPA, state data breach notification laws)
- **International data considerations** -- awareness of Mexico's Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP) to the extent member/unit holder data is processed in Mexico

### 10.2 Data Ownership

- Member/unit holder data is owned by the Company
- Transfer Agent holds data as a fiduciary and service provider
- Upon termination, Transfer Agent must transfer all records to successor within 30 days
- Transfer Agent may not use member/unit holder data for any purpose other than providing services to the Company

## 12. Term & Termination

### 12.1 Term

- **Initial term:** 12 months from execution
- **Renewal:** Automatic annual renewal unless terminated
- **Termination for convenience:** 90 days written notice by either party
- **Termination for cause:** 30 days written notice with opportunity to cure

### 12.2 Transition Obligations

Upon termination:

1. Transfer Agent cooperates in transition to successor
2. Complete member/unit holder records transferred within 30 days
3. Transfer Agent provides data in standard electronic format
4. Transfer Agent continues processing transactions during transition period
5. Final fees invoiced and settled within 60 days
