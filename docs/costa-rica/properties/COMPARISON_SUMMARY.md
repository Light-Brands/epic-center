### The Medical Access Dilemma

All five properties share the fundamental Costa Rica challenge: no JCI hospital within 30 minutes. The scoring reflects this reality, but it's important to note that this challenge is **shared by ALL competing facilities in the region**, including Rythmia (which has operated safely for 10+ years).

**Mitigation Strategy (applies to any chosen property):**
1. **On-site medical team:** Full-time physician + 2 nurses + cardiac monitoring equipment (~$300K/yr)
2. **On-site emergency bay:** Equipped stabilization room with O2, crash cart, cardiac monitoring (~$150K setup)
3. **Air evacuation contract:** Helicopter service to CIMA San Jose (~$8-15K/flight; annual retainer ~$50K)
4. **Ground ambulance protocol:** Cruz Roja pre-arranged rapid response
5. **Guest screening:** Enhanced pre-arrival medical screening to reduce emergency probability
6. **Medical director oversight:** Board-certified physician directing all treatment protocols

**Cost of medical mitigation package:** ~$500K setup + ~$400K/year operating

This investment is necessary regardless of which property is chosen and should be factored into all financial models.
