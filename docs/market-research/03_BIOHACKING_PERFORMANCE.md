## 2. Core Modalities Analysis

### 2.1 Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT)

**Mechanism:** Breathing pure oxygen in pressurized chamber increases tissue oxygenation

**Research Evidence:**
- Telomere lengthening observed in clinical studies
- Senescent cell clearance demonstrated
- Cognitive enhancement in aging populations
- Wound healing acceleration proven

**Equipment Costs:**
- Soft chamber (mild): $5,000 - $15,000
- Hard chamber (clinical): $75,000 - $250,000
- Multi-place chamber: $500,000+

**Session Pricing:** $100 - $300 per session

**Protocol:** Typically 60-90 minute sessions, 20-40 session protocols

---

### 2.2 Cryotherapy

**Types:**
- Whole Body Cryotherapy (WBC): -110°C to -140°C for 2-3 minutes
- Localized Cryotherapy: Targeted cold application

**Benefits Claimed:**
- Reduced inflammation
- Enhanced recovery
- Endorphin release
- Metabolic boost
- Skin improvement

**Equipment Costs:**
- Single-person cryo chamber: $40,000 - $75,000
- Multi-person walk-in: $150,000 - $400,000

**Session Pricing:** $25 - $100 per session

---

### 2.3 Red Light Therapy / Photobiomodulation

**Mechanism:** Specific wavelengths (630-850nm) stimulate cellular mitochondria

**Benefits:**
- Skin health and collagen production
- Wound healing
- Pain reduction
- Athletic recovery
- Hair growth stimulation

**Equipment Costs:**
- Personal panels: $300 - $1,500
- Professional full-body beds: $20,000 - $100,000

**Session Pricing:** $25 - $75 per session

---

### 2.4 Infrared Saunas

**Types:**
- Near-infrared (NIR): 700-1400nm
- Mid-infrared (MIR): 1400-3000nm
- Far-infrared (FIR): 3000nm-1mm
- Full-spectrum: All wavelengths

**Benefits:**
- Deep tissue heating
- Detoxification via sweating
- Cardiovascular conditioning
- Relaxation and stress relief
- Pain management

**Equipment Costs:**
- Home units: $2,000 - $8,000
- Commercial units: $10,000 - $50,000

**Session Pricing:** $30 - $60 per session

---

### 2.5 Float Tanks / Sensory Deprivation

**Environment:** 1,000+ lbs Epsom salt solution, body temperature, light and sound proof

**Benefits:**

- Stress reduction
- Pain relief

**Equipment Costs:**

**Session Pricing:** $60 - $100 per 60-90 minute float
