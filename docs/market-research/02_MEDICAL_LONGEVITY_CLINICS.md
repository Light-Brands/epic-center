## 4. Emerging Longevity Technologies

### 4.1 Senolytics
- Drugs that clear senescent cells
- Dasatinib + Quercetin protocols
- Fisetin supplementation
- Still largely experimental

### 4.2 Rapamycin & mTOR Modulation
- Intermittent dosing protocols
- Immune system effects
- Requires medical supervision

### 4.3 Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT)
- Telomere lengthening studies
- Senescent cell clearance
- Cognitive enhancement
- Session cost: $100-300

### 4.4 Young Blood/Plasma Exchange
- Controversial but emerging
- Limited clinical evidence
- Regulatory challenges
