## 3. Protocol Duration Analysis

### 3.1 Common Program Lengths

| Duration | Use Case | Price Range |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| 3-4 nights | Introduction/assessment | $2,000-5,000 |
| 7 nights | Standard programs | $5,000-15,000 |
| 10-14 nights | Deep transformation | $10,000-30,000 |
| 21+ nights | Comprehensive change | $20,000-50,000+ |
| 28+ nights | Addiction/trauma recovery | $50,000-150,000+ |

### 3.2 Optimal Durations by Goal

**Wellness Reset:**
- Minimum: 4 nights
- Recommended: 7 nights
- Optimal: 10-14 nights

**Detoxification:**
- Minimum: 7 nights
- Recommended: 14 nights
- Optimal: 21 nights

**Addiction Treatment (Ibogaine):**
- Minimum: 7 days
- Recommended: 10-14 days
- With integration: 21+ days

**Comprehensive Transformation:**
- Minimum: 14 nights
- Recommended: 21-28 nights
- Optimal: 28+ nights

### 3.3 Pre-Care & Post-Care Considerations

**Pre-Care (typically included or additional):**
- Medical questionnaires
- Telehealth consultations
- Supplement protocols
- Dietary preparation
- Cost: Often included or $500-2,000 additional

**Post-Care/Integration:**
- Follow-up consultations
- Supplement continuation
- Lifestyle coaching
- Alumni programs
- Cost: $2,500-10,000+ for comprehensive aftercare

## 5. Payment & Financing Options

### 5.1 Common Payment Structures

**Deposit Requirements:**
- Typical: 25-50% at booking
- Balance: 30 days before arrival
- Cancellation policies vary

**Payment Methods:**
- Wire transfer (preferred for large amounts)
- Credit card (often 3% surcharge)
- Cryptocurrency (emerging, select centers)

### 5.2 Financing Programs

**Healthcare Financing:**
- Medical loans (CareCredit, Prosper)
- Interest-free options (12-18 months)
- Medical credit cards

**Concierge Medicine Models:**
- Annual membership fees
- Monthly payment plans
- Retainer arrangements

**Insurance Coverage:**
- Generally not covered
- Some HSA/FSA eligible expenses
- Medical tourism insurance available

## 6. Pricing Psychology & Tier Structures

### 6.1 Common Tier Approaches

**Bronze/Silver/Gold Model:**
| Tier | Characteristics | Price Multiplier |
|------|----------------|------------------|
| Bronze | Core program, standard room | 1.0x |
| Silver | Enhanced program, upgraded room | 1.5x |
| Gold | Premium program, suite, extras | 2.0x |
| Platinum | All-inclusive, private, concierge | 2.5-3.0x |

**Duration-Based Tiers:**
- Longer stays = lower per-day rate
- Incentivizes extended programs
- Better outcomes with longer protocols

### 6.2 Anchoring Strategies

**Premium Anchor:**
- Lead with highest-priced option
- Makes mid-tier seem reasonable
- Example: Show $48,000 premium first

**Value Comparison:**
- Compare to alternatives (surgery, ongoing treatment)
- Lifetime value framing
- "Cost per day of transformation"

### 6.3 Early Booking & Referral Programs

**Early Booking:**
- 10-15% discount typical
- SHA: 15% for 60+ days advance
- Secures commitment, aids planning

**Referral Programs:**
- Client referral credits ($500-2,000)
- Alumni discounts for return visits
- VIP tiers for repeat clients

### 6.4 Corporate & Group Pricing

**Corporate Wellness:**
- Executive retreat packages
- Company wellness programs
- Volume discounts available

**Group Programs:**
- 10-20% discount for groups
- Private retreats available
- Customized programming
