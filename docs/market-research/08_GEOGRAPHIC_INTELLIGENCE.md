# Geographic Intelligence Analysis
## Optimal Locations for a Luxury Medical & Plant Medicine Transformation Clinic

*Last Updated: January 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

Location selection is a critical strategic decision that impacts legal viability, client accessibility, operational costs, and competitive positioning. This report analyzes priority regions for establishing a luxury medical and plant medicine transformation clinic.

**Top Recommendation:** Mexico (Quintana Roo or Baja California) for combination of legal framework, accessibility, infrastructure, and operational advantages.

**Secondary Options:** Portugal (European market), Costa Rica (wellness reputation), Jamaica (psilocybin focus)

---

## 1. Priority Region Analysis

### 1.1 MEXICO

#### Legal Status

**Ibogaine:**
- **Status:** Unregulated (legal)
- Ibogaine is not scheduled in Mexico
- Operates like over-the-counter natural remedy
- No government restrictions on clinical use
- Clinics operate openly with medical supervision

**Other Substances:**
- Ketamine: Legal for medical use
- Psilocybin: Traditionally used, gray area
- Ayahuasca: Traditional use tolerated

**Regulatory Environment:**
- Lack of formal regulation = flexibility
- Also means variable quality across providers
- Self-regulation through medical protocols essential
- No anticipated change in near term

### 1.3 PORTUGAL

#### Legal Status

**Drug Decriminalization (since 2001):**
- Personal possession decriminalized (all drugs)
- 10-day supply threshold
- Trafficking still illegal
- Not full legalization

**Ibogaine:**
- Unregulated (not scheduled)
- Operates in decriminalized environment
- Clinics function with medical oversight

**Psychedelics:**
- Possession decriminalized
- Not commercially licensed
- Clinical research expanding

| Real Estate | ★★★★☆ | Moderate |
