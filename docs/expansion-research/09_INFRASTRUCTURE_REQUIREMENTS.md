# Infrastructure Requirements

> *Medical, Operational, and Facility Standards for Global Expansion*

---

## Overview

This document specifies the infrastructure requirements for Transformational Epicenter facilities across all tiers. These requirements ensure safety, quality, and brand consistency across the global network.

---

## Part 1: Facility Tier Specifications

### 1.1 Tier Summary

| Tier | Capacity | Investment | Locations | Description |
|------|----------|------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Tier 1: Flagship** | 15-20 guests | $40-60M | 5 | Full four-pillar integration, ICU capability |
| **Tier 2: Regional Hub** | 10-15 guests | $20-35M | 10 | Three-pillar integration, hospital partnership |
| **Tier 3: Satellite** | 6-10 guests | $10-20M | 15 | Specialized focus, emergency protocols |

### 1.2 Tier 1: Flagship Requirements

**Capacity:** 15-20 private guest rooms

**Land Requirements:**
- Minimum 1.5 hectares (3.7 acres)
- Privacy from public areas
- Natural setting (ocean, forest, mountains, or gardens)
- Expansion potential to 2.5+ hectares

**Building Requirements:**
- Main treatment center: 2,500-3,500 m² (27,000-38,000 sq ft)
- Guest villas: 50-80 m² each (540-860 sq ft)
- Bio-optimization center: 500-800 m² (5,400-8,600 sq ft)
- Dining and social areas: 400-600 m² (4,300-6,500 sq ft)
- Staff facilities: 300-500 m² (3,200-5,400 sq ft)
- Support buildings: 200-400 m² (2,150-4,300 sq ft)

**Medical Infrastructure:**
- On-site ICU capability (2-4 beds)
- 24/7 physician coverage
- Crash cart with full cardiac equipment
- Continuous monitoring systems
- Emergency helicopter landing zone

**Bio-Optimization Suite:**
- Hyperbaric oxygen chambers (2-3 units)
- Cryotherapy chamber
- IV therapy stations (4-6)
- Float tanks (2-3)
- Infrared sauna suite
- Cold plunge and contrast therapy
- Neurofeedback stations (2-4)
- PEMF and red light therapy
- Sleep optimization pods

### 1.3 Tier 2: Regional Hub Requirements

**Capacity:** 10-15 private guest rooms

**Land Requirements:**
- Minimum 0.75 hectares (1.85 acres)
- Privacy from public areas
- Natural setting

**Building Requirements:**
- Main treatment center: 1,500-2,500 m² (16,000-27,000 sq ft)
- Guest rooms: 40-60 m² each (430-650 sq ft)
- Bio-optimization center: 300-500 m² (3,200-5,400 sq ft)
- Dining and social: 250-400 m² (2,700-4,300 sq ft)
- Staff facilities: 200-300 m² (2,150-3,200 sq ft)

**Medical Infrastructure:**
- Medical suite with monitoring capability
- Physician on-call (15-minute response)
- Crash cart with cardiac equipment
- Hospital partnership (within 30 minutes)
- Emergency transport protocols

**Bio-Optimization Suite:**
- Hyperbaric oxygen chamber (1-2 units)
- Cryotherapy or cold plunge
- IV therapy stations (3-4)
- Float tank (1-2)
- Infrared sauna
- Core bio-optimization equipment

### 1.4 Tier 3: Satellite Requirements

**Capacity:** 6-10 private guest rooms

**Land Requirements:**
- Minimum 0.5 hectares (1.2 acres)
- Privacy from public areas

**Building Requirements:**
- Main building: 1,000-1,500 m² (10,800-16,000 sq ft)
- Guest rooms: 35-50 m² each (375-540 sq ft)
- Treatment/wellness space: 200-300 m² (2,150-3,200 sq ft)
- Dining and social: 150-250 m² (1,600-2,700 sq ft)

**Medical Infrastructure:**
- Medical room with basic monitoring
- Nurse/paramedic on-site
- Physician on-call
- Hospital within 45 minutes
- Emergency protocols and transport

**Bio-Optimization Suite (Selective):**
- IV therapy stations (2)
- Infrared sauna
- Cold therapy
- Core equipment based on specialization

### 2.4 Pre-Treatment Medical Protocols

**Required Screening (All Guests):**

| Test | Timing | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Complete Blood Count | Pre-arrival | Baseline health status |
| Comprehensive Metabolic Panel | Pre-arrival | Organ function, electrolytes |
| Liver Function Tests | Pre-arrival | Metabolism capability |
| Electrocardiogram (12-lead) | Pre-arrival + on-site | Cardiac baseline, QTc |
| Echocardiogram | Pre-arrival (high-risk) | Structural assessment |
| Thyroid Panel | Pre-arrival | Metabolic status |
| Urinalysis + Drug Screen | On-arrival | Substances in system |
| Pregnancy Test (if applicable) | On-arrival | Absolute contraindication |

**Ibogaine-Specific Requirements:**

| Criterion | Standard | Action if Failed |
|-----------|----------|------------------|
| QTc Interval | Men <450ms, Women <470ms | Exclude from ibogaine |
| Electrolytes (K+) | 4.5-5.5 mEq/L | Correct before treatment |
| Electrolytes (Mg2+) | 1.5-2.5 mEq/L | Correct before treatment |
| Liver Function | Within normal limits | Dose adjustment or exclude |
| Cardiac History | No structural disease | Cardiology clearance required |

## Part 5: Safety and Security Infrastructure

### 5.1 Emergency Response

**On-Site Requirements:**

| System | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Emergency power generator | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Fire suppression system | Full | Full | Basic |
| Emergency lighting | Full | Full | Full |
| Emergency communication | Satellite backup | Backup system | Local |
| Helipad/helicopter access | Yes | Recommended | If remote |
| Ambulance parking/access | Yes | Yes | Yes |

**Emergency Protocols:**
- Written emergency response procedures
- Regular staff training and drills
- Hospital transfer protocols
- Guest emergency contact procedures
- Crisis communication plan

### 5.2 Physical Security

**Tier 1 Flagship:**
- Perimeter security (fencing, natural barriers)
- 24/7 security personnel
- Controlled access points
- Guest and staff identification
- Vehicle screening
- CCTV at perimeter and common areas

**Privacy Considerations:**
- No cameras in guest areas
- Sound isolation between spaces
- Private entrances where possible
- Staff confidentiality training

### 5.3 Natural Disaster Preparedness

**Location-Specific Requirements:**

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Hurricane (Caribbean, Mexico) | Storm-rated construction, evacuation plan, supplies |
| Earthquake (Baja, Costa Rica) | Seismic construction standards, emergency supplies |
| Flooding | Elevated construction, drainage systems |
| Fire | Fire-resistant materials, suppression, evacuation |

**Standard Requirements:**
- Emergency supplies (72+ hours)
- Evacuation procedures and routes
- Guest and staff assembly points
- Communication protocols during events

- [ ] Zoning confirmed for medical/hospitality use

- [ ] Utility availability verified
- [ ] Hospital proximity confirmed
- [ ] Transportation access verified

**Design:**
- [ ] Architect with healthcare experience engaged

- [ ] Medical gas systems designed

- [ ] Telemetry infrastructure installed

- [ ] Proper ventilation for treatment rooms

**Hospitality Systems:**
- [ ] Commercial kitchen to code
- [ ] Guest room technology infrastructure
- [ ] Sound isolation verified
- [ ] Climate control tested
- [ ] Water systems functional

**Medical:**
- [ ] All medical equipment delivered and tested
- [ ] Hospital partnership formalized
- [ ] Emergency protocols documented
- [ ] Staff credentialing complete
- [ ] Medical supplies stocked

**Operational:**
- [ ] Technology systems tested
- [ ] Staff training completed
