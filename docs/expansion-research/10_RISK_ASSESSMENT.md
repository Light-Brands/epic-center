# Risk Assessment Matrix

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive assessment of risks associated with the 30-location global expansion strategy. Each risk is evaluated for probability, impact, and mitigation approaches.

## Part 7: Contingency Planning

### 7.1 Scenario Response Plans

**Scenario 1: Regulatory Prohibition**
- Trigger: Government announces scheduling of ibogaine
- Response: Activate alternative location protocols
- Timeline: 90-day transition plan to redirect guests
- Communication: Proactive guest notification, rebooking to alternate facility

**Scenario 2: Major Safety Incident**
- Trigger: Serious adverse event or fatality
- Response: Crisis communication plan, investigation, protocol review
- Timeline: Immediate (24 hours) initial response
- Communication: Prepared statements, media coordination, family liaison

**Scenario 3: Natural Disaster**
- Trigger: Hurricane/earthquake warning or event
- Response: Evacuation protocols, guest safety, business continuity
- Timeline: Based on warning time
- Communication: Guest notification, family contact, status updates

**Scenario 4: Economic Crisis**
- Trigger: Significant demand reduction (>30%)
- Response: Cost reduction measures, pricing flexibility, marketing pivot
- Timeline: 90-day implementation
- Communication: Staff notification, investor communication
