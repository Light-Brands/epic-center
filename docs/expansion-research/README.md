### Key Selection Criteria (Weighted)

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Regulatory Environment | 25% | Legal status of plant medicine |
| Medical Infrastructure | 20% | Hospital quality, emergency capability |
| Market Accessibility | 15% | Flight connections, visa requirements |
| Real Estate Viability | 15% | Land availability, costs, zoning |
| Talent Availability | 10% | Medical, hospitality workforce |
| Climate & Environment | 10% | Year-round suitability, natural setting |
| Operational Costs | 5% | Labor, taxation, supply chain |

### Non-Negotiable Requirements

All locations must satisfy:

1. **Legal Framework**: Plant medicine legal, decriminalized, or unregulated
2. **Medical Infrastructure**: JCI-accredited hospital within 30 minutes
3. **Airport Access**: International airport within 90 minutes
4. **Political Stability**: Stable governance environment
5. **Security Level**: US State Department Level 1 or 2 only
