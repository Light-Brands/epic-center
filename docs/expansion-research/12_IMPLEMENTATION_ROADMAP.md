## Part 8: Risk Contingencies

### 8.1 Schedule Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Contingency | Trigger |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Construction delay | Accelerate other locations | >2 months behind |
| Permit delay | Alternative sites identified | >3 months delay |
| Funding gap | Reduce scope or delay phase | <6 months runway |
| Talent shortage | Expand recruiting geography | Key roles unfilled 60+ days |

### 8.2 Go/No-Go Decision Points

**Phase Transition Gates:**

| Gate | Criteria | Decision |
|------|----------|----------|
| Phase 1 → 2 | Flagship profitable, 2+ locations operating, Series B secured | Proceed/Delay |
| Phase 2 → 3 | 8+ locations, network EBITDA positive, Series C/D secured | Proceed/Delay |
| Phase 3 → 4 | 15+ locations, $100M+ revenue, clear path to profitability | Proceed/Delay |
