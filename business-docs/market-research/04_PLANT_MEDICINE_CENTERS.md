## 6. Safety Considerations

### 6.1 Ibogaine Risks

**Medical Concerns:**
- QT prolongation (cardiac)
- Potential for fatal arrhythmia
- Drug interactions
- Contraindicated conditions:
  - Heart disease
  - Liver impairment
  - Certain medications

**Safety Requirements:**
- Comprehensive cardiac screening
- ECG monitoring
- Emergency equipment
- Medical staff on-site 24/7
- Drug clearance protocols

### 6.2 Ayahuasca Risks

**Physical:**
- Purging (vomiting, diarrhea)
- Hypertensive crisis (with MAOIs)
- Serotonin syndrome potential

**Psychological:**
- Challenging psychological experiences
- Potential trauma emergence
- Not suitable for psychosis history

**Safety Requirements:**
- Medication screening
- Mental health assessment
- Experienced facilitators
- Integration support

### 6.3 Best Practices

1. **Medical Screening** - Comprehensive health assessment
2. **Drug Interactions** - Full medication review
3. **Cardiac Monitoring** - Especially for ibogaine
4. **Qualified Staff** - Medical professionals present
5. **Emergency Protocols** - Hospital access, equipment
6. **Integration Support** - Pre and post-treatment
7. **Informed Consent** - Clear risk communication
